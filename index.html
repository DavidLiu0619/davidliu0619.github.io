Biography

Hengyuan is a senior transfer student at the University of California, Davis. He is going to receive a B.S. in Statistics by the end of the Spring Quarter of 2023 and he is planning to apply to graduate school for an M.S. in Statistics. His dream job is to be a data analyst. In 2019, he attended the Sacramento City College, and he found that he was passionate about theory, inference, and the application of Statistics. The rigorous study of data analysis has given him the opportunity to model and solve real-world problems statistically. He began to prepare to transfer to the Department of Statistics at UC Davis. In 2021, he earned the certification of Intersegmental General Education Transfer Curriculum (IGETC). To develop his statistics analysis skill, he completed Calculus, Introduction to Statistics, and Programming C/C++ in SCC. Based on his outstanding academic achievement, he gained the President’s Highest Honors in Fall 2019. After transferring to UC Davis, he dived into the upper-division statistics courses including Linear Algebra, Linear Regression, Analysis of Variance, Probability Theory, and Nonparametric Statistics. He led one of the Nonparametric Statistics courses’ projects to compare the hours of pain relief for two common over-the-counter pain medications to find the optimal medical treatment. Through the project, he was interested in analyzing methodology and R coding skills to solve a practical medical problem. At UC Davis, Hengyuan is participating in the ROTC military program and Davis Data Science Club so that he can learn and exercise his leadership. Now, he is working on a project about analyzing the Tercero Dining Hall data. Also, he is serving in the Living-Stone Fellowship in Davis Chinese Christian Church. Additionally, he is the guitar player in the Sunday Mandarin Worship and he is one of the leaders of the Bible Study Group. In his spare time, he enjoys playing basketball and working out in the gym. He likes to go to the library and read books. He also likes to watch American movies to practice his English.

About Me

Hengyuan is a driven Master of Applied Statistics and Data Science major at the University of California, Los Angeles, with a strong academic background in Statistics, having earned his B.S. from the University of California, Davis. Immigrating to the United States with his family in 2017, Hengyuan's dream job is to become a data scientist. His passion for theory, inference, and applying Statistics to real-world problems was ignited during his time at Sacramento City College, where he prepared to transfer to UC Davis. His outstanding achievements, including the President's Highest Honors, reflect his dedication to academic excellence.
